Model Display

Eligibility Criteria for Event: Model Display

 Junior - Class 6 to 8
 Senior - Class 9 to 12


Rules:

1. All participants should have a valid ID proof (School ID Card).
2. Maximum number of 4 participants are allowed per team.
3. Each team should display their model throughout the event schedule. Failing this, a team may be liable for disqualification.
4. Participants should bring their requirements (Extension cord etc.) with them on the day of the event.
5. The decision of the judges will be final and no dispute will be entertained.
6. Rules may be changed without prior notice to the participants.
