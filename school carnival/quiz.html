Event-Quiz

Eligibility Criteria for Participating the Quiz Event - Class 8 to 12

Rules:

1. All participants should have a valid ID proof (School ID Card).


2. Each team should contain a maximum of two participants.


3. An initial screening round on general knowledge will be conducted to select six teams for final rounds of quiz competition.


4. A total of four rounds will be conducted out of which two teams will be eliminated after the first two rounds.


5. Questions for the different rounds will be based on current affairs, audio visual clips, sports,mythology, culture, movies and music.


6. Winner, first runner up and second runner up prizes will be given to the participants along with participation certificates to all.
