Sit & Draw

Eligibility Criteria for Event: Sit & Draw

Class 6 to 8


Rules:

1. All participants should have a valid ID proof (School ID Card).
2. Only drawing paper will be supplied to each participant.
3. Other necessary materials like pencil, rubber, colour etc. are to be brought by individual participants. Any medium of colouring is welcome viz. watercolour/pastel/coloured pencil, pencil shading etc.
4. Use of any kind of pen (sketch/fountain/gel/highlighter etc.) is strictly not allowed to draw/colour. However, it can be used only for writing captions/messages/comments etc. in the drawing sheet.
5. All participants will be awarded a certificate of participation along with a small refreshment packet.
6. There will be three winners (1st, 2nd & 3rd place) and they will be awarded with Memento and Winners Certificate.
7. Decisions of the Jury Panel will be final and binding.


Topic (any one):

1. Save earth, stop pollution
2. Character from your favourite book
3. Say NO to plastic
4. A mythological figure
5. Some place you've dreamt about
6. Social justice for all, end child labour
7. Pencil-shaded portrait
