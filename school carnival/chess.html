Event-State Level Chess Tournament

Eligibility Criteria for Event: State Level Chess Tournament

[Separate events for Boys & Girls]
Junior - Class 6 to 8
Senior - Class 9 to 12


Rules:

1. All participants should have a valid ID proof (School ID Card).
2. Registration will be done on a first come first serve basis and Maximum 150 - 200 players are allowed to participate the event.
3. System: The tournament will be conducted as per FIDE Laws of Chess and FIDE Swiss System with 5 or 6 rounds.
4. Time Control: Session 40 Min. (Each 20 Min.)
5. Electronic Devices: All electronic items including all types of wrist-watches are not allowed in the tournament hall.
6. The Chief Arbiter's decision is final and binding on all.
7. Withdrawal: A player may withdraw at any stage of the event, after informing the Chief Arbiter in writing.
8. The decision of the organizing committee shall be final and binding on all parties in all matters pertaining to the tournament.
9. Students should maintain discipline.
