Carrom Tournament

Eligibility Criteria for Participating the Carrom Tournament:


 Junior - Class 6 to 8
 Senior - Class 9 to 12

Rules:


1. All participants should have a valid ID proof (School ID Card).
2. Red is not allowed in hit stroke.
3. Always due fine considered if there is no available coins (the carrom pieces).
4. At the stroke time if any player's body touches any coins and then the coin is pocketed, then two coins considered as fine.
5. If any player touches any moving coin or striker after the stroke then single coin is considered as fine.
6. For single line, one coin is considered as fine.
7. Single fine and double fine as considered for one coin and two coin.
8. The judgement will decided after 3 boards.
9. Push at the time of stroke considered as a fine of single coin.
10. Shoulder and elbows must always stay outside of the playing board area.
11. Playing outside the proper zone is considered as a fine of one coin.
12. After one stroke is taken, the next stroke must be taken within 30 seconds.
13. Any issues of jumping of coins will be decided by the judges.
14. Player can play using both hands.
15. If any player use personal striker then it must be verified before playing by the judge.
16. The decision of judge is final and binding. The match structure will be decided on the day of the event by the organizers. No disputes will be entertained.
