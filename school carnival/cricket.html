Inter School Cricket Tournament

Eligibility Criteria for Event: Inter School Cricket Tournament

[9 Players per Team]

Class 6 to 12


Rules:

1. All participants should have a valid ID proof (School ID Card).
2. The basic rules of short cricket are applicable for this event.
3. Each team should consist of 9 players.
4. All Players of the team should be considered from same school.
5. Each team should carry their own bats, while the College will provide tennis balls only during matches.
6. Each innings will have a maximum of 6 overs play.
7. Umpire's decision is final.
8. Players can hit and score 4; hitting 6 is not allowed. If anyone hits 6, it will be considered as out.
