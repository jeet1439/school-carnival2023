General-rules

❖ Registration process will be closed 30 minutes before the commencement of each event.


❖ Decisions of the jury will be final and binding for all events.


❖ Any or all the rules are subject to change at any point of time.


❖ Participation certificates will be provided to all the registered participants.


❖ Students interested in taking part in the events should register on the day of the carnival at the registration committee.


❖ There is no registration fee.


❖ A participant can participate in any number of events as long as the event timings do not clash. In case of simultaneous timings of events, a participant can only participate in one of the clashing events.


❖ All the events will commence at the scheduled time. Punctuality will be appreciated.

❖ All the participants should be in proper school uniform.


❖ All participants should have proper ID card for proof of date of birth and should be able to produce the same in original when asked for verification, failing which they will not be considered for any award and the prize will be awarded to the participant next in the merit list.


❖ Use of any unfair means will lead to disqualification.
