Eligibility Criteria for Participating the Extempore Event:


 Junior - Class 6 to 8
 Senior - Class 9 to 12

Extempore means without planning or preparation. In this event, a candidate is asked to speak briefly on a given topic without preparation beforehand. Clarity of thought, range of knowledge, presence of mind and fluency of a candidate are generally examined in a

Rules:


1. All participants should have a valid ID proof (School ID Card).
2. Participants may speak in any of the three languages, ENGLISH, BENGALI or HINDI.
3. Each participant will be given 4 minutes to speak on a topic.
4. Topics will be randomly selected by means of lottery.
5. A maximum of 30 seconds may be given to a candidate for preparation after knowing the topic mentioned in the lottery chit.
6. A participant will be allowed to select the topic once only.
7. Participants will not be allowed to take any paper or material with them.
8. Participation certificates will be given to all participants.
Judgement:
1. Judges will look for clarity of thought, range of knowledge, presence of mind, effective use of language, and body language.
2. The decision of the Jury Panel will be final and binding.
3. First, second and third place winners will be awarded mementos and certificates.
